AI Ping Pong Game









Instructions

1. Press the Start Game button to begin
2. To move your paddle use your wrists. To move up, Direct your wrists upwards and downwards to lead your paddle down
3. Adjust your paddle to make sure you hit the ball back to the otherside.
4. You will win points if you hit the ball back, but if you miss the point goes to the computer.
GOOD LUCK!
